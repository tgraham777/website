About Me

Background:
I have a BSBA with a major in finance from the University of Denver and also completed the one year MBA program at DU with a concentration in finance and investment management. I graduated with Honors and Distinction in June 2012. I have experience with financial research, portfolio management, mortgages, credit, marketing, and sales.

Programming:
In February 2015 the investment company that I work for offered to sponsor me to go to programming school in order to turn the economic and investment models I was working on into usable software. While the school primarily focuses on the Ruby and Rails languages, we also learn everything from Sinatra to JavaScript. My anticipated graduation date is December 2015, at which point I will begin working on a way to evaluate Federal Reserve economic models. The goal is to write a program that can automatically update the models with new data and to provide a user-friendly interface for employees and clients to access and view that information.
