What is so cool about galaxies, you might ask? Here are some fun facts:

  • A galaxy is a massive group of stars, star clusters, interstellar gas and dust that is bound together by gravity. Scientists now think that galaxies also contain large amounts of a mysterious gravitational source known as "dark matter."
  • The word 'galaxy' is derived from the Greek word galaxias which means "milky," in reference to our own Milky Way galaxy.
  • There are potentially more than 170 billion galaxies in the observable universe. Each galaxy can contain anywhere from 10 million to 100 trillion stars!
source: http://www.sciencekids.co.nz/sciencefacts/space/galaxies.html
